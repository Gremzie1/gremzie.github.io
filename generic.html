From Me to You

Dear My Friends,

It is through you and because of you that I've been so lucky as to be able to put together stupid shit like this. I never imagined I would be in a position where no matter how bad of a day I have, I can come home and know I have people around me filled with love and support. I want to say thank you to each and every one of you who has ever decided that I was worth keeping around.
Let's make December 13th, my birthday, a night that is special to all of us.
Thank you so much,
Gremzie.
